How MediNexus Works

1
Real-Time Data Ingestion
Vitals monitors, bed sensors, energy meters, and patient systems feed live data every 2 seconds into the MediNexus pipeline.
2
Multi-Agent AI Analysis
5 specialized AI agents analyze data in parallel — patient safety, bed flow, energy, patient care, and orchestration.

CAPABILITIES

5 Agents, Infinite Intelligence

SentinelAgent — Patient Safety
Monitors all patient vitals in real-time, calculates Early Warning Scores (EWS), detects sepsis risk, fall risk, and medication conflicts 2-4 hours before they become critical.
FlowAgent — Bed & Resource Management
Tracks real-time bed occupancy, predicts discharges using ML models, optimizes staff allocation, and prevents bottlenecks before they occur.
GreenAgent — Energy Optimizer
Intelligently manages HVAC, lighting, and non-critical equipment. Delivers 15-25% energy savings per shift without impacting patient care quality.
CareGuide — Patient Companion
AI conversational assistant for patients in Hindi, Tamil, and English. Handles wayfinding, medication questions, appointment reminders, and anxiety support.
CommandAgent — Orchestrator
Coordinates all agents, prioritizes alerts, routes to correct staff, maintains audit logs, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
Human Oversight & Trust
Every critical action requires clinician approval. Full audit trail. Configurable alert thresholds. AI assists — never replaces — human judgment.
