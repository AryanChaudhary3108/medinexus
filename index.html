How MediNexus Works

1
Real-Time Data Ingestion
Vitals streams, bed state updates, and patient systems feed live data into the MediNexus pipeline every few seconds.
2
Parallel AI Analysis
Specialized AI models analyze data in parallel for patient safety, bed flow, care guidance, and orchestration.
3

4
Human-in-the-Loop

CAPABILITIES

Unified Intelligence Platform

Patient Safety Monitoring
Monitors all patient vitals in real-time, calculates Early Warning Scores (EWS), detects sepsis risk, fall risk, and medication conflicts 2-4 hours before they become critical.
Bed & Resource Management
Tracks real-time bed occupancy, predicts discharges using ML models, optimizes staff allocation, and prevents bottlenecks before they occur.
Clinical Context Engine
Brings patient notes, pending labs, and operational context together so teams can make fast, well-grounded decisions from one shared view.
Patient Companion
AI conversational assistant for patients in Hindi, Tamil, and English. Handles wayfinding, medication questions, appointment reminders, and anxiety support.
Operations Orchestrator
Coordinates all intelligence modules, prioritizes alerts, routes to correct staff, maintains audit logs, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
Human Oversight & Trust
Every critical action requires clinician approval with complete audit history and role-based accountability. AI assists, humans decide.
